Our Timeline Journey

2002

Founded by 5 professionals, full-skilled and ICT enthusiast, with various background. First project was developing Hospital Integrated System for the biggest state hospital in Jakarta.

2005

Started developing the flagship product; SCASY School Academic System. Establishing an in-house framework for development named MUNTILAN. It has been used to develop dozens of application solutions.

2007

Granted several awards for the company in-house product and innovation. Indonesia ICT AWARD, INAICTA, and more, as the acknowledgment from the Indonesian Government and International organizations for our hard work and dedication.

2010

Expanding the business by cooperating with some high-performance ICT products principals from all over the world.

2011

Entering IOS and Android mobile app development with hybrid and native programming language.

2013

Providing Managed Services for Content Management and  Updating for mobile applications and Quality Assurance services for mobile applications, CMS and web applications.

2020

Started venturing in the blockchain development for traceability and transaction recording.

2023

Expanding business to create Artificial Intelligence Algorithms as the use of AI become more and more common in the digital world.
